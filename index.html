What is Churn Rate?

Churn rate measures the percentage of subscribers who cancel within a given time frame. Lower churn supports better retention, LTV, and profitability.

Why Reducing Churn Matters

  • Stronger Loyalty: Customers stay longer
  • Lower CAC Payback: More value from each acquisition
  • Stable Revenue: Less volatility in subscription income
  • Higher LTV & Margins: Longer relationships = stronger economics
